About Me

Biography

Dual‑major PhD candidate in Computational Linguistics and Middle Eastern Languages & Cultures at Indiana University Bloomington, with a minor in Computer Science. Research targets NLP for Arabic and cross‑lingual settings with an emphasis on natural language inference.

Background in linguistics supports system design and evaluation for Arabic and related languages. Portfolio covers dialect identification, sexism detection, ellipsis detection, sentiment analysis, and public‑opinion analysis. MA work in corpus linguistics examined syntax and emotion in narratives of major and bipolar depression.
